Design Rationale

Carbon fiber chosen because the end effector travels to 93" — every gram saved at height reduces tipping risk and enables lower gear ratios for faster movement.

Gear-driven design eliminates belt tension and chain skip — zero adjustments needed mid-competition.

MAXSpline shaft routes all wiring internally — eliminates wire twist over 720° of rotation.

Iteration History
V1 — "Trident" (Rejected)
Angled front plate for ground pickup. No compliance made ground pickup nearly impossible. Narrow acquisition zone made alignment very difficult.
V2 — 4481-Style Inside Gripper
Inspired by Team 4481 Rembrandts (OpenAlliance). 3D-printed polycarbonate gearset. Spring-loaded algae claw added. MAXComposite SRPP plates on 2.125×1 CF tube.
V3 — Final (Refined 4481 Style)
Algae claw removed (unnecessary weight, strategy revised). Chamfered gear edges reduce edge loading. Final configuration throughout competition season.
