Worlds Upgrade — Alignment Funnel

For championships, a funnel was added to reduce cage alignment time. One hard side (aluminum) and one soft side (polycarbonate) pushes the anchor away from the end effector during approach.

Asymmetric compliance guides the cage pipe into the grip teeth even with imprecise approach angles — reducing driver workload in the final 30 seconds.

Iteration History
V1 — Rusthounds RI3D Design (Rejected)
Two MAXSpline pipe design, spring hook, servo release, dyneema winch. Too many moving parts, servo damage, limited clearance with elevator, difficult cage alignment.
V2 — Everybot Door-Hinge Design (Rejected)
Door hinge rotation point, 4-plate polycarbonate one-way gate, ratchet wrench winch. Rejected — packaging conflicts, insufficient frame space.
V3 — Team 2630 Two-Finger Design (Final)
Two fingers engage single cage pipe. ⅛" aluminum superstructure twisted under load — FEA confirmed ¼" needed. Forks iterated to ⅜" steel with geometry matched to cage curvature.
