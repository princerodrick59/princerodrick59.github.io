Indexer

Moves fuel from the spindexer to the shooter. The only iteration was a material switch from aluminum to SRPP to reduce weight. A 1.9:1 reduction minimizes jamming, and the rolled stainless steel backer gives fuel a consistent rigid surface to roll against.

1792 TubeRoller System

Every roller on the robot uses our custom standardized dead-axle TubeRoller system — a 3D-printable, competition-durable roller compatible with PVC or polycarbonate tubes, fully parametric for any robot.

A ¼-20 bolt replaces a traditional shaft, eliminating bending risk and reducing weight. Carefully spaced washers eliminate bearing face contact so rollers spin freely. The integrated pulley hub enables direct power transmission without additional components.
