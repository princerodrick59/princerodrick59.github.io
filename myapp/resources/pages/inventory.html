Inventoria — Digital Inventory System

We utilize Inventoria to maintain a fully itemized digital inventory of all components, including their location and subsystem association within our shop. This system ensures we remain match-ready by enabling rapid identification and retrieval of parts, allowing us to make quick and effective repairs during competition.

SoftwareInventoria
Tracking FieldsPart Name, Location, Subsystem
AccessShop-Wide (All Members)
Primary GoalRapid Pit Repair Capability
2025 Expansion — Barcode Scanning

In 2025, we expanded the system by integrating barcode scanning into our pit workflow. This allows us to scan entire replacement subsystems and instantly log or track them within Inventoria, significantly streamlining the process of swapping components during events.

Shop Usage & Procurement

In the shop environment, the inventory system supports efficient part sourcing and procurement by helping us locate existing inventory and identify components that need to be ordered, improving both organization and overall team efficiency.

1
Locate Existing Parts
Every part has a logged shop location. Students can find what they need without hunting through bins — reducing build time lost to searching.
2
Identify What to Order
Low-stock components are visible in the system before they become a critical shortage during build season or at competition.
3
Subsystem Association
Parts are tagged to their subsystem, making it easy to pull everything needed for a repair or rebuild without missing components.
